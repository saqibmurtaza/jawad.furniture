Furniture Terminology

Accent Tables

Accent tables are small, decorative tables used to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of a room. They complement the decor and can be used for various purposes, such as displaying decorative items or holding drinks.

End Tables

End tables, also known as side tables, are small tables placed next to sofas or beds. They provide surfaces for lamps, books, or beverages within easy reach.

Dining Sideboard

A dining sideboard, or buffet, is a long, low piece of dining room furniture used for serving food, storing dining essentials, and displaying decorative items.

Ottoman

An ottoman is a versatile piece of furniture, often upholstered and cushioned, used as a footrest or extra seating. It can also have storage compartments under its top surface.
