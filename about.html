J's Furniture

After completing my business studies, I started my career as a Banker in 1996. I worked in different departments of banks and was promoted to AVP. My banking experience spans over 10 years, after which I switched my career and started business. I established a furniture manufacturing unit in 2006 with the name of J’s furniture.

Since we started, our mission has always been to provide our clients with the highest quality products and customer service, while maintaining a completely competitive price.

J’s proudly design and develop an entire range of furniture products in Pakistan. The upholstered products, which include sofas, chairs, ottomans, sofa-beds, and beds, are also exclusive to J's range.

We combine creativity, craftsmanship and technology to create furniture suited for discerning customers, locally and internationally.

We have gained considerable experience dealing with diverse customers including designers, architects, professionals, and both local and foreign customers.
