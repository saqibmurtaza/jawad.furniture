Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the history of J's Furniture?

J's Furniture was established in 2006 by our founder, who had over 10 years of experience in the banking industry. The company's mission has always been to provide high-quality furniture products and exceptional customer service while offering competitive prices.

2. What types of furniture does J's Furniture offer?

J's Furniture offers a wide range of furniture products, including sofas, chairs, ottomans, sofa-beds, and beds. Our upholstered products are exclusive to the J's Furniture range.

3. What sets J's Furniture apart from other furniture manufacturers?

We distinguish ourselves by combining creativity, craftsmanship, and technology to create furniture tailored for discerning customers, both locally and internationally.

4. Who are your typical customers?

We serve a diverse customer base, including designers, architects, professionals, and both local and foreign customers.

5. What are your payment terms for orders?

We require a 50% advance payment to proceed with orders. The full payment is due upon delivery.

6. How can I place an order with J's Furniture?

You can contact us via phone, WhatsApp, email, or by visiting our factory to place an order. We encourage discussing and finalizing the specifications of your items through messages as evidence to avoid any design inconveniences.

7. Is the advance payment refundable if I cancel my order?

Unfortunately, the advance payment is non-refundable if an order is canceled.

8. Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we provide delivery services throughout Pakistan. The delivery charges are borne by the client.

9. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via bank transfer, EasyPaisa, Jazz, and cash, whichever is convenient for the client.

10. Who is responsible for any breakage during delivery?

The owner is not responsible for any breakage that occurs during delivery.

11. What should I do to ensure the proper specifications of my furniture order?

It's important to provide written specifications before placing an order to avoid any misunderstandings. The owner is not responsible if proper specs are not conveyed.
