J's Furniture

Payment Terms

A 50% advance payment is required to initiate the production of furniture orders. The remaining 50% of the payment must be made upon the delivery of the furniture.

Order Placement

Orders can be placed through various channels, including phone, WhatsApp, email, or by visiting our factory in person. We encourage customers to discuss and finalize the specifications of their furniture items through written communication to avoid any design discrepancies.

Furniture Specs

It's important to provide written specifications before placing an order to avoid any misunderstandings. The owner is not responsible if proper specs are not conveyed.

Advance Payment Policy

The advance payment for furniture orders is non-refundable in the event of order cancellation.

Delivery Services

We offer nationwide delivery services across Pakistan. Delivery charges are the responsibility of the client and will be borne by the client.

Note: The owner is not responsible for any breakage that occurs during delivery.
